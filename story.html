IN CALENDAR YEAR 2008,

UNI SUN WAS RESIDING IN A ZEN TEMPLE IN JAPAN.

LATER THAT YEAR, AFTER CONFIRMATION OF ENLIGHTENMENT, HE LEFT THE TEMPLE.

HE DECIDED THAT HE WOULD START ANEW AFTER THE TEMPLE, AND THUS LET GO OF ALL PREVIOUS CONTACTS.

HE ULTIMATELY DECIDED TO ONLY MAKE NEW RELATIONSHIPS, TO BETTER GUARANTEE COMPLETE PURITY AND DEDICATION.

ALTHOUGH THIS WAS A MAJOR SACRIFICE, HE WANTED TO HONOR THE EXPERIENCE AT THE TEMPLE.

HE DECIDED TO STAY COMPLETELY TRUE TO HIS VOW AND DEDICATION TO ABSOLUTE TRUTH.

 

HE  MAINTAINS A MEATLESS VEGETARIAN (BORDERLINE VEGAN) DIET AND DOESN'T USE DRUGS, INCLUDING ALCOHOL.

HIS BODY IS LITERALLY HIS TEMPLE.

HE DOES NOT WATCH TELEVISION, DOES NOT READ NEWSPAPERS, AND MAINLY WATCHES EDUCATIONAL CONTENT WHEN WATCHING VIDEOS.

ALTHOUGH HE HAS DEALT WITH MANY HARDSHIPS BECAUSE OF HIS VOW AND DEDICATION, HE HAS STAYED TRUE TO HIS VOW.

BY NOT RELYING ON HISTORICAL CONTACTS AND RELATIONSHIPS, HE LEARNED TO BE STRONG AND HONED NUMEROUS SKILLS.

 

HE HAS RESIDED AROUND THE WORLD AND HAS BEEN ON A SERIAL ENTREPRENEUR PATH FOR THE PAST DECADE.

HE DESIGNED AND ENGINEERED LEADING EDGE PROTOTYPE SW AND HW PRODUCTS FOR MULTIPLE STARTUPS.

 

HIS FIRST LOVE IN THIS WORLD IS BEAUTIFUL NATURE, YET HE HAS BEEN HONING AN URBAN TEMPLE PRACTICE THAT HE CALLS CITY ZEN.

HE USUALLY WEARS ALL BLACK CLOTHING, BECAUSE THAT IS HIS URBAN TEMPLE ROBE.

HIS  "NORMAL" DAY HAS BASICALLY BEEN RISING, MEDITATING, BATHING, EATING, DRINKING, WORKING 8-12 HOURS, AND GOING TO BED AGAIN.

UNI SUN'S MAIN FOCUS HAS GENERALLY BEEN TO STAY AS PERFECT AS POSSIBLE AS CONTINUALLY AS POSSIBLE.

HE HAS WORKED AND TRAINED TO BE THE BEST HE CAN BE.
